Lab fee: $125. ##Course Requirements ❖ Research Presentation : Students must have at least one 10 to 15 minute presentation, which will be discussed along with a reading response ❖ Weekly Assignment : Students will upload a small project on your personal website using the technique they learned in class every week. activate your website http://www.buffalo.edu/ubit/service-guides/web-hosting/creating-a-personal-website/getting-started-with-your-per sonal-website.html Semester Projects ( sketch or proposal + project ) 1. A ‘performing program’, i.e. it has a discernible behavior, resulting in some kind of generating graphics, drawing patterns or repeating vector animation. Thus it attracts attention and hopefully has some kind of personality. 2. A ‘responsive object’ that reacts with changeable behaviors in response either to actions/changes in its immediate environment, or in response to data or events somewhere else. This tends to convey the impression that it is a type of living thing, as we are familiar with many animals that respond differently depending on what’s going on around them, how they are treated, etc.
